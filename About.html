WHO WE ARE

One of the eminent names in the Cattle Feed Manufacturing
Industry, the company has state of the art facilities at
Ahmednagar with a manufacturing capacity of 540 metric
tonnes of livestock feed per day. We diligently make it a point to
source high quality ingredients in order to be able to produce
premium and superbly nutritious livestock feed so that your
animals give the best performance.

We strongly believe that - ‘true success lies in the progress of
our customers, suppliers, business partners and the
community.’ Our day – to – day operations are based on the
same ethos. Supported by our core values of integrity and
transparency, we provide our customers with a wide range of
animal nutrition solutions to help them gain profits in their businesses.

Quality Assurance

We produce high quality animal feed at our modern plant following stringent quality norms at each step starting from procurement of raw material to the final dispatch of finished product.

High Delivery Capacity

With the most avant garde technology, equipment and raw material sourcing process, Kanhaiyya Agro promises a very high delivery capacity of livestock feed in suitable environment in order to maintain its quality.

Warehousing

We have a spacious, high capacity warehousing unit to store the products safely until they are dispatched. Our warehouse is equipped with temperature controlling system to store the animal feed.
